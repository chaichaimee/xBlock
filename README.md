## **The Ultimate Productivity Powerhouse**

**xBlock** is a professional-grade NVDA add-on built for users who value efficiency. It transforms how you handle repetitive text by creating a structured library of snippets. Whether it's complex code, standard email replies, or frequently used forms, xBlock ensures your content is organized and ready to be deployed at the speed of thought.

<br>

## **Key Commands**

> [!TIP]
> **Windows + F11:** Open the xBlock Dialog.
>
> *To change this shortcut, go to **NVDA Menu > Preferences > Input Gestures**, and search for "xBlock" under the Global Plugins category.*

<br>

## **How It Works: Step-by-Step**

1. **Launch:** Press **Windows + F11** to open the xBlock Command Center.
2. **Organize:** Choose the **"All"</b> category or click **"New Category"** to create a custom group for your blocks.
3. **Create:** Enter a **Block Name** (the title you'll see in the list) and type or paste your text into the **Block Content** field.
4. **Save:** Click the **"Add"** button to store your new snippet.
5. **Paste:** Simply select your block from the list and hit **Enter**. The window will close, and your text will be instantly pasted into your active application.

<br>

## **Advanced Capabilities**

* **Smart Categorization:** Effortlessly manage hundreds of blocks by grouping them into dedicated categories.
* **Priority Management:** Right-click any block to **Pin** it to the top. Use **Move Up/Down** to arrange your most important snippets exactly where you want them.
* **Seamless Editing:** Right-click to edit content, move blocks to different categories, or delete unwanted items instantly.
